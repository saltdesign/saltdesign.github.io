About Us

Salt Electrical which started trading in 1995 by Marcus Salt with a few portable tools, basic equipment and a little Bedford Rascal van, began portable appliance testing (PAT) and small installations until getting several poultry house installations.

Becoming self employed with the ethos of customer service, the business grew and new employees were added to the workforce and trained to form a team of highly dedicated staff. We provide services to customers such as Moy Park Foods Ltd, Donnelly Horticulture, Skea Eggs to name but a few.

With our knowledge of Electrical & Ventilation installations in the Poultry industry including fully automatic systems featuring our unique rack & Pinion systems, we have diversified into the ever expanding fields of Horticultural, Commerical and Industrial.

We pride yourselves in providing our customers with project design, Installation, management, maintenance and 24/7 back up service second to none. Using nothing but the best in materials and products from suppliers of a exceptional business reputation such as Multifan , Ridder Drives, Belimo, Opticon, W.S.Mercers and C.F Whaler etc.
